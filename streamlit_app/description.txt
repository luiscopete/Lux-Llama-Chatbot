<p style="text-align: justify; font-size: 14px;">
Lux is an advanced chatbot application developed using Streamlit. This AI assistant is designed to interact with users in a conversational manner, providing intelligent responses based on various LLM models, including Llama, and Flan. Lux is capable of understanding and responding in multiple languages.
Key features include sentiment adjustment, allowing the assistant to tailor its tone and country-specific settings to better align responses with regional contexts. The chatbot supports both text and speech input, enhancing user interaction through voice commands.
